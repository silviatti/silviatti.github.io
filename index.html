About me

Hello there! I'm Silvia Terragni and I'm a third-year Ph.D. Student at the University of Milano-Bicocca. My research focuses on Topic Models and NLP. I am currently collaborating with the D2K lab at EURECOM on the topic of topic modeling evalutation.

I've been also collaborating with MilaNLP Lab on the combination of neural topic models and language models' representations. I am currently a active maintainer and contributor of the python package contextualized-topic-models.

If you want to know more about my project and stuff, here you can currently find a small selection of my projects and my publications . Otherwise we can get in touch on Twitter or by email :)
